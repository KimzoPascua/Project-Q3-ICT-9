                      🔥🔥🔥🔥🔥🔥🔥🔥What is Fire prevention month?🔥🔥🔥🔥🔥🔥🔥🔥

March is Fire Prevention Month in the Philippines, making it an ideal time to discuss and raise awareness about the causes, hazards, and dangers of fire. The month of March was labeled as "Fire Prevention Month" or "Burn  According to the measure, the fire prevention month campaign promotes "safety consciousness among our people every day of the year as a positive preventive approach to a problem that can be solved by more caution, vigilance, sobriety, common sense, and respect for the law."The dry season is leading to a rise in reported fires across the nation.March is one of the hottest months of the year, and it marks the start of the Philippine summer season. According to Bureau of Fire Protection data, it is also the month with the most alarming fire incidents in the country (BFP).

ELECTRICAL WIRING ALWAYS check your wires for any damages to avoid sparks to cause any fire. Do often checking of wires such as chargers,extentions cords to fix it right away! "A faulty wire can start a fire"
CANDLES More than half of these candle-related home fires began when a candle was placed too close to flammable material, such as a curtain or drape. Mattresses and bedding are some of the most common combustible materials ignited by candles, resulting in house fires. Blinds or curtains.
FIRE EXTINGUISHER Having Fire extinguihser in the household can be a life saver
COOKING Kitchen is where usually where fire starts this happens when the ur food isn't supervised, not turning off the applainces after using it Cooking oil and grease fires are one of the most common types of kitchen fires. The most important thing to remember is that putting it out with water will only spread the fire. Instead, first turn off the burner. Because oxygen promotes fire, deplete it by covering the cooking pan with a metal lid.
FLAMMABLE MATERIALS Flammable materials must be kept away from children such as rubbing alcohol, cooking oil, and etc this is to prevent any harm doing to any children at home
SMOKE DETECTOR A smoke detector must be own every family in their home this works by detecting light reflected off particles in the sensing chamber from a light beam. When there are no particles in the sensing chamber, the light from the beam does not strike the light detector, indicating that everything is fine.This will alarm everyone that there is a fire started so you can evacuate safely
